Welcome to L'Antipasto City

Your destination for authentic Italian dining in the heart of London. Located at 2 Botolph Alley, just moments from the iconic Tower of London, our restaurant blends traditional Italian flavors with a contemporary twist. From handcrafted antipasti and fresh pasta to rich, slow-cooked mains and indulgent desserts, every dish is prepared with passion and premium ingredients. Whether you're stopping by for a business lunch, a relaxed dinner, or a special occasion, our warm, elegant setting and attentive service create the perfect dining experience. At L’Antipasto City, we bring the heart of Italy to the city , one plate at a time.
